Bolton Strid

Rafting the Bolton Strid sucks you and the raft underwater, where whirlpools and vorticies thrash you against sides and you are squeezed through a variety of hidden cavens for an exhilirating ride. Eventually, you reappear safely about 10 metres down stream.

Destinations
Site: 4. Bolton Strid Itinerary
Finance
  1. Travel to Leeds and take the bus to the Devonshire Arms Hotel and take the night to refresh
  2. In the morning travel to Howgill and strat rafting there
  3. It should take an hour until you get to the strid
  4. As you arrive at the strid, hold breath as you get sucked under the water (be sure you can hold your breath for 1 minute)
  5. Raft the underwater currents, which has been estimated to be up to 70 metres deep
  6. Then reappear safely 10 meters downstream
  7. Continue on until you get to Boton Priory to see the sights
  8. Then continue on until you raft back to Devonshire Hotel
