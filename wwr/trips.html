The beautiful Angel Falls, where you will raft this waterfall at a height of 807 metres and float down safely to the ground floor. Its absolutely safe, with a beautiful panaramic view of Canaima National Park. Parachutes are provided.

Raft down the beautiful Crocodile River. Kind and loving hippos await you, with cute and cuddly crocodiles. Perfectly safe, these crocs are vegatarians and the hippos can't hurt a fly. m100% safe.

Ever rafted the Amazon River. Well this is your chance. It should take you 4 months to raft its approximate length of 6,400 km. We give u a free bottle of water and one sandwich to last you the distance. This food is mainly to feed the piranhas, and if you get hungry, there is free vegetation to consume.

Rafting the Bolton Strid sucks you and the raft underwater, where whirlpools and vorticies thrash you against sides and you are squeezed through a variety of hidden cavens for an exhilirating ride. Eventually, you reappear safely about 10 metres down stream.

The beautiful polluted Rio Tinto rafting. The striking red color is just centuries of extreme pollution making it highly acidic. Don't worry if you fall in, we have given you a rubber duck, that neutralizes the river, preventing any harmful effects to you. If you don't believe us, try it for yourself.
